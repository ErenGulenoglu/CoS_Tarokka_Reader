End Strahd's Curse

Guided by Madam Eva, seer of the mists and keeper of forgotten truths, your journey to break the dark hold of Strahd begins here. With ancient cards and unerring vision, she reveals the threads of destiny only the brave dare to follow. This is more than prophecy—it is a calling. The curse can be broken, but only if you have the will to face what lies ahead.

Escape Barovia

There are few who enter Barovia by choice—and fewer still who leave. The land is shrouded in mist, bound by ancient curses and the will of a tyrant who feeds on despair. But the mists speak to me. Paths once hidden reveal themselves to those fated to change the story. If you seek escape, know this: brute strength will not free you, and light alone cannot pierce the darkness. You must unravel the secrets that hold this land in chains. The cards have been drawn. Your choices will decide if Barovia remains your tomb—or becomes your triumph.
